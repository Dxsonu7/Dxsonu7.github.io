Intro

I am Sonu Gupta, a first-generation college student from Nepal. I study Computer Science at Northwestern College. Growing up as a minority in terai regions of Nepal, I learned to rejoice the differences, diversity, and cultures among us. It has been an overwhelming, heartwarming, and bittersweet journey for me – travelling to the United States for higher education after receiving scholarships. There have been transitions but my love for technology, representing my culture, and giving back to community has never changed, instead, there has been significant growth. As a result, in college I contribute as one of the leaders of Intercultural-Core (I-Core) and manage Intercultural page with a positive vision of making our campus community ‘more beloved’! Moreover, I work as an IT-technician (upcoming IT student team lead 2023!) where I support and provide IT-related assistance to students and faculties. Besides that, in my spare time, I support high school students in Nepal assisting them in categories including but not limited to insights about college education, college search, application and guidance, technology advise, and mentoring. To learn more about me, you can check out my works/experiences.
